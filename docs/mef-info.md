Marai El Fassi
Independent Consultant for Natural Resource Governance and Climate Policy

Intro
I provide consultancy services at the intersection of natural resource governance, climate policy, sustainability and international development.
I support governments, international organizations and NGOs in evaluating programs, developing projects and generating evidence for more effective climate and sustainability policies.
My work combines monitoring and evaluation, qualitative and participatory research, policy advisory, facilitation of multi-stakeholder processes, and project development.

Core Services

1. Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL) – Independent evaluations and monitoring systems for climate and development programs, including OECD-DAC based assessments.
2. Climate & Sustainability Policy Advisory – Evidence-based policy advice on climate adaptation, natural resource governance, ecosystem-based approaches and socially inclusive sustainability policies.
3. Qualitative Research – Participatory and qualitative research to generate evidence for climate, sustainability and development initiatives.
4. Project Development – Development of projects, policy briefs and knowledge products for international donors and organizations.

Selected Experience

Climate & Sustainability Policy Advisory
Evidence-based policy advice on climate adaptation, natural resource governance and sustainability.
I support institutions and decision-makers in developing policies and strategies that address climate risks, promote sustainable resource management and integrate social and gender perspectives.

Services include
• Climate adaptation strategies
• Ecosystem-based adaptation
• Nature-based solutions
• Climate and health policies
• Gender analysis
• Gender mainstreaming in climate policies
• Inclusive sustainability policy design

Selected Experience
• Policy advice on heat resilience and climate adaptation in the health sector in collaboration with municipalities and health institutions.
• Contribution to the Fourth Gender Equality Report of the German Federal Government on gender equality in the socio-ecological transformation.
• Research and policy engagement on ecosystem-based adaptation within international climate policy networks.

Qualitative Research
Participatory and qualitative research to generate evidence for climate, sustainability and development initiatives.
My work focuses on understanding social, environmental and governance dynamics through participatory methods and stakeholder engagement.

Services include
• Participatory research design
• Stakeholder interviews and focus groups
• Qualitative data analysis
• Community-based assessments
• Knowledge synthesis for policy and program development

Selected Experience
• Applied research on ecosystem-based climate adaptation in Guatemala, including qualitative field research and case studies with communities and partner organizations.
• Stakeholder interviews and participatory workshops on climate adaptation, water governance and development policies in Latin America and Europe.
